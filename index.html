Innova Research

We are a Singapore-based research team afflicated to X-Lab, SenseTime Research. Thriving to bring together computer vision and computer graphics, our research interests include 3D Vision and Virtual Humans.
We offer full-time/internship positions for talented individuals who look forward to expanding the boundary of human knowledge and/or transforming ideas into commercial values. PhD opportunities are also available in collaboration with world-leading universities for prospective students.
Contact us at innova@sensetime.com.
